Determination and Resilience: Raising Leaders in a War-Torn Country

Type: Written testimonial

Source: Personal communication from Rachel Moss

Date: Not specified

Description

This testimonial recounts the life of Naw Plaw Htoo, a Karen woman who endured decades of conflict while raising children and supporting her community in Karen State. The story begins with her escape from a village attack while pregnant, culminating in childbirth while hiding in the jungle from advancing soldiers.

Within this archive, her narrative demonstrates how resilience often unfolds across generations through family, faith, and the raising of future leaders.
