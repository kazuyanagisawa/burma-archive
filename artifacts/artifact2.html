Medic Testimony

Context

This interview features a Karenni medic describing their work in IDP camps during the ongoing conflict in Myanmar. The testimony documents both the physical and emotional demands of providing care in a crisis zone.

Curatorial Note

The medic's account illustrates how humanitarian workers navigate danger, scarcity, and grief while maintaining a commitment to care. The testimony reveals the human cost of conflict as experienced by those providing aid.

Connection to the Project

This artifact demonstrates the limitations of speech-to-text transcription when applied to testimony in Burmese. Manual correction was required to preserve the integrity of the speaker's words and meaning.
