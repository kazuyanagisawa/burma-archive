Child Letter

Context

This handwritten letter was written by a child in a displaced community. The message expresses gratitude, affection, and religious faith despite ongoing conflict and displacement.

Curatorial Note

The letter demonstrates how children use familiar emotional language to maintain relationships during instability. Blessings, expressions of love, and references to faith appear throughout the message.

Connection to the Project

This artifact illustrates how testimony can survive even when mediated through imperfect OCR and translation. While the digital transcription contains errors, the emotional structure of the letter remains legible.
