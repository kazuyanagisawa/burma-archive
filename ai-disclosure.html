AI Disclosure

I used AI extensively throughout this project. I used GitHub Copilot, Claude, Gemini, and especially Codex in VS Code to help generate and revise HTML, make project-wide code edits, and assist with phrasing, restructuring, and revision of written prose. I extensively edited the outputs for accuracy, tone, structure, and interpretation, and all final selections, arguments, and wording decisions are my own. Perplexity and Consensus were used for research using a variety of search terms included below. I also used LLMs for proofreading using the prompt below.

Prompts

Do not substantially rewrite, expand, or alter my interpretation. Limit revisions to minor corrections and light stylistic smoothing so that the writing retains its academic integrity and remains fundamentally my own.”

“Your job is to create three new artifact pages in the same style and structure as the sample page I provide below. Follow the existing site’s formatting conventions closely, but you may simplify slightly if needed.
